Character Bios

Harry Potter

The Harry Potter film series follows the journey of a young wizard, Harry Potter, as he confronts the dark wizard Lord Voldemort. Set in a magical world, it showcases themes of friendship, bravery, and the battle between good and evil.

Spider-Man

Spider-Man has appeared in Hollywood films across three franchises: Sam Raimi's trilogy with Tobey Maguire, The Amazing Spider-Man series with Andrew Garfield, and the MCU with Tom Holland. Each series explores Peter Parker's journey as Spider-Man, battling iconic villains while balancing his personal life.
